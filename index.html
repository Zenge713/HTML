I grew up in a small town called Alvin, which is located south of Houston, Texas. I would frequent the beaches of Galveston to go skateboarding because the area was so nice.

Although I was a graduating junior, I was allowed to walk with the class of '08 so I was surrounded by my friends. I still somehow managed to be within the top 15% of the class! I did find that graduating early was extremely boring and eventually left my hometown to travel and work on the pipeline with my dad.

The relevance of the Jack Black picture is warranted as I'm often mistaken as Jack Black when I grow my hair out. It can definitely make for a good conversation starter! Not only that, but in hindsight it would make dressing up for Halloween pretty easy.

After years of hard work and not really happy with my job prospects, I decided to go back to college and do what I had always wanted to do but never followed those dreams. That dream is taking me down the path of coding, and with all things considered that 2020 has brought... I would dare to say it's turned out to be a rather exciting year.
